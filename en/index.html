Who she is

She is not a born triathlete.

She became what she is — and you can see it in her. Not talent. Work, repetition, will. A Brazilian woman who came to Berlin and learned that the body can do more than you give it credit for.

From a half marathon in 2023 to the leap into the Mediterranean. From the tarmac of Tempelhof Field to the World Championship in Nice. Five heroes inside her: the seeker of vastness, the wild one, the just one, the one who sees through, the one who laughs. And a question on 12 September: what can a person achieve when everything comes together.
